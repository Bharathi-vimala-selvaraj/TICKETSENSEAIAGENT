# 🎫 AI Ticket Triage Agent

### 🚀 Developed by Team SentraFix

An Intelligent AI-Powered Customer Support Assistant for Automated Ticket Classification, Sentiment Analysis, Urgency Detection, and Response Generation.

---

## 📌 Overview

AI Ticket Triage Agent is a smart customer support automation platform that helps organizations efficiently manage support requests by automatically analyzing incoming tickets.

The system leverages Artificial Intelligence to:

- Classify support tickets
- Detect customer sentiment
- Determine urgency levels
- Generate professional response drafts
- Provide actionable insights for support teams

By automating the ticket triage process, support teams can focus on solving customer issues rather than manually reviewing and prioritizing requests.

## ⚠️ Problem Statement

Customer support teams often handle hundreds or thousands of tickets every day.

Manual ticket triage creates several challenges:

- Support agents spend valuable time reading and categorizing tickets.
- Critical issues may not receive immediate attention.
- Delayed responses negatively impact customer satisfaction.
- Recurring customer pain points remain hidden within unstructured ticket data.

---

## 🎯 Our Solution

AI Ticket Triage Agent acts as an intelligent first responder.

The platform automatically:

- Classifies support tickets
- Detects customer sentiment
- Assigns urgency levels
- Generates response drafts
- Provides support analytics
- Improves ticket management efficiency

## ✨ Key Features

### 📂 Automated Ticket Classification

Automatically categorizes tickets into:

- Billing Issues
- Account Management
- Product Feedback
- Feature Requests
- Customer Complaints
- General Queries

### 😊 Sentiment Analysis

Analyzes customer emotions and classifies them as:

- Positive
- Neutral
- Negative

### 🚨 Urgency Detection

Determines ticket priority levels:

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

### 🤖 AI Response Generation

Generates professional and context-aware draft responses.

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard

Provides:

- Ticket Volume Analysis
- Sentiment Distribution
- Urgency Trends
- Category Breakdown
- Support Insights

### 💾 Smart Data Management

Stores and processes ticket information efficiently for reporting and analytics.
